Hi there! My name is Neil. I am a self taught programmer, web developer and games developer with a true passion for coding. I first got into coding around 24 months ago when i was looking around trying to turn my childhood passion, Video Games, into an income stream. Since then i have explored many aspects to do with video games development and found that my true passion lies in coding. I have taught myself all the fundamentals of Full Stack Web Development using the MERN stack as well as Unity, Aodbe Suite, Video Editing, Sound Engineering and other things necessary to make games. With all that said, i am an avid student of the coding arts who is looking to bring his life experience to a company in a coding role where i can learn from people in the field and broaden my horizons. While i may not have a formal education, i bring a wealth of life experience, and even more passion, that will be a great asset to any company.
